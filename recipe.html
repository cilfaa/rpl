Ingredients

  • White Wine Vinegar3 tbsp
  • Free-range eggs4 units
  • Toasting Muffins2 units
  • Parma Ham4 units

For the hollandaise sauce

  • Butter125 g
  • Egg yolks2 units
  • White wine vinegar1/2 tsp
  • Lemon juice-
  • Cayenne pepperpinch

How to Make

STEP 1
Bring water to boil, add vinegar, lower heat to gentle simmer.

STEP 2
Crack eggs into cups. Toast muffins lightly.

For hollandaise sauce

STEP 1
Melt butter, skim solids, keep warm.

STEP 2
Whisk egg yolks with vinegar over simmering water.

STEP 3
Slowly whisk butter, add lemon juice and cayenne.

Poached eggs

STEP 1
Swirl water, slide egg in, cook 2 - 3 minutes.

STEP 2
Assemble muffins, ham, egg, sauce. Serve.
